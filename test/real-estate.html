We are representing companies and individuals in every type of real estate related transactions, including:


Primary legal investigation and RESEARCH QUESTIONS:

  • The valuation of the property
  • Checking the property if it is registered in the land registry, or has no mortgages attached to it
  • The parties will undertake every procedure that is officially required or needed in practice to transfer the ownership of the property
  • Cadastral/Mapping system
  • Land Registry record keeping
  • Does the law require that all property sale transactions be registered at the land registry to make opposite to third parties?
  • Is the system of immovable property registration subject to a guarantee?
  • Is every privately held land plot in Serbia formally registered at the immovable property registry?
  • Can they accessed by interested parties?
  • Is the registration of mortgage required by law?
  • Does the law require that all property sale transactions be registered at the land registry to make opposite to third parties?

LIST OF PROCEDURES FOR TRANSFERRING PROPERTY:

  • If the seller company has accepted the buyer company’s offer to purchase the property:
    We are helping client, to purchase a commercial or residential space!
    See more at: Mergers & Acquisitions
  • Complete the process for transferring a property
  • The buyer or seller legal representing before Republic Geodetic Authority – Service for Cadastre of Immovable Property
  • Prepare the sale contract and solemnize it by Public Notary office.
  • Pay the price and monitoring money transferring - domestic or foreigner
  • Submit the request for registration to the Cadastre
  • Submit the application to Tax Authorities for the approval of the transaction value and for the authorization of the transfer tax payment
  • Pay the transfer tax, etc. See more at: Tax services
