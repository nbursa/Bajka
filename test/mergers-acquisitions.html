Our legal services:

Professional practice in representation: from start-up, participation in various transactions to legal services during the regular course of business.

We provide consultations on mergers and acquisitions between domestic and foreign companies as well as multinational legal entities.

Our M&A practice provides a wide range of transactions, tender offers, exchange offers and capital venture.
See more at Zastupanje prodaja firmi - manjinski/većinski

We are handling complex commercial corporate transactions for companies undergoing M&A and cross-border transactions as well as investments in a wide range of industries, trade, properties and investments in hi-tech (“IT“) companies, etc.

We have a team of experienced lawyers, consultants providing quality counseling, helping clients to find the best possible solution for structuring business deals.
See more at: Associate Partners

We represent strategic investors, as well as target companies in business acquisitions and provide counseling on related state regulatory issues, including government restrictions, company law and local employment law.
See more at: Labor & Employment


Our lawyers and financial advisors represent clients in the following areas:

  • legal & financial structuring of transactions
  • negotiations
  • due diligence reports
  • corporate meetings
  • preparing legal transaction documents
  • purchase agreements
  • drafting business contracts(sale/acquisition agreements, management status issues, company reorganization, employments status)
  • protecting minority – majority owners
    • legal question for a private limited companies:
      • Does the sale of 51 % or more of Buyer’s assets require the consent of the majority of its members?
      • Must members of Buyer meet once a year?
      • Must annual financial statements of Buyer be audited by an external auditor?
      • Can members who represent 10 % of Buyer’s capital call for a meeting?
      • Must all members of Buyer consent to add a new member?
      • Must a member of Buyer first offer to sell his interest to the existing members before selling to a non-member…etc? See more at: Company registration
    • Legal questions for Listed Companies - publicity traded listed corporations:
      • Does the sale of 51% or more of Buyer’s assets require shareholder approval?
      • Must Buyer disclose its audit reports to the public?
      • Can shareholders who hold 10% of Buyer’s share capital call for an extraordinary meeting?
      • Must the board of directors (or supervisory board= include independent and non-executive board members?
      • Can shareholders remove members of the board of directors without cause before the end of their term?
      • Can shareholders or members who hold 5% of Buyer’s share capital put items on the general meeting agenda …etc? See more at: Company registration
  • tax consulting
    See more at: Tax services
