Permanent representation - a mountly base retainer


Permanent lump-sum legal representation

 

Attorneys from our Office have more than 20 years of professional experience in domestic, regional and international economy.

Professional practice in representation: from start-up, participation in various transactions to legal services during the regular course of business in the territory of the Republic of Serbia.

Cooperation with numerous domestic, foreign and mixed-ownership companies yielded a form of proficient, cost-effective and generally accepted model of long-term cooperation with our clients specific to the market conditions in the Republic of Serbia.

We recommend to all companies and entrepreneurs signing an Agreement on lump-sum legal representation: “monthly base retainer contract“.


Why...?

Reasons, motives, advantages and benefits:

  • Clients get permanent – daily professional legal assistance based on the coverage:
    24h a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year (24 /7/365).

 

Lump-sum legal representation provides precautionary, primary legal protection with the aim of solving problems at their inception or preventing further negative consequences for the Client, which can be in the status of: employer, creditor, debtor, petitioner or defendant...

Lump-sum legal representation is individual, price-adjusted according to the number of employees, types of services rendered and particular needs of a certain company.

 

Client's Rights:

 

The Client is being represented before all domestic, foreign, natural and legal persons, as well as state bodies or Courts.

You get a team of experienced attorneys, jurists, consultants – always available and at disposal.

Inclusion of all necessary experts – tax and financial advisors, after a joint assessment of a particular case.

Permanent communication with the Client is technically conducted in several ways:

  • By phone, e-mail, directly at meetings in our office, seat of the Client as well as by conference calls - virtual office (Skype, Viber).
  • Meetings are possible even at your business partners in the territory of Belgrade, and as per special agreement – out of town or abroad.

The right to unlimited number of calls and updates:

  • In the case of urgent needs, the same day replies even after working hours, during holidays and weekends.

 

Services included in the lump-sum legal representation:


  • Legal protection of the Employer in view of legal obligations of employees
  • Compulsory Employer's provisions for all employees
    See more at: Labor & Employment

Tasks and procedures for adoption of decisions by company's bodies: holding of Assembly sessions in accordance with the law and company's acts.

Oral or written interpretations, opinions, instructions, analyses and advices concerning the application of laws in the business sphere of the Client.

Business contracts: compensations, transfers, assignments, cessions, assignations, etc.

Checking of legal and liquidity status of companies with which the Client intends to cooperate:

  • in courts, Tax Administration, National Bank of Serbia, Serbian Business Registers Agency, etc.
  • procedures of liquidation and bankruptcy.

Consultations during selection of financial and legal security of payments:

  • bill of exchange, registration and realization of pledges, mortgages, contracts with guarantor, etc.

Status changes in the Serbian Business Registers Agency: change of the name, seat, address, activity, etc.

Procedures for acquiring protection in the sphere of industrial property rights:

  • protection of trademark; copyrights, etc.

Legal representation in the process of collecting claims:
See more at: Debt collection

Protection of interests during various inspections: environmental, sanitary, market, customs, financial police and in the case of filed minor offence charges.

Representation in proceedings before Commercial and Administrative Courts and state institutions:
See more at: Court litigation

Representation in public procurement procedures(tenders);
See more at: Javne nabavke – tenderi

Procedures for obtaining permits, licenses, certificates from state institutions.

Representation of foreign citizens in the process of obtaining temporary resident status in the Republic of Serbia before the immigration police – Ministry of Interior.
See more at: Labor & Employment

After the finalization of the registration procedure we could provide you a draft Contract for our legal services on a monthly basis.

Confidentiality clauses are compulsory in the application of the Agreement.

 

Price

Standard costs of the lump-sum representation

 

Lump-sum legal representation services are paid monthly, as a proposed fixed price – fee for all legal services in the course of the current month of legal representation:

  • "lump-sum legal representation price" is on average below an average net monthly salary of the lawyer employed in the company;
  • it is also below the fee of an experienced attorney!

A company with a signed lump-sum legal representation agreement has no need for its own legal service and is thus drastically reducing costs for the future, urgent engagement of an attorney.

- our team shall always have enough time for you and the necessary quality of service.

Calls and verbal consultations are never charged separately or calculated per hour, tariffs or the number of calls with the Client!

Representation in other – regular court proceedings is offered at far more favorable conditions compared to regular fees!


The Client acquires priority status in legal representation on the basis of contracted long-term cooperation!


Note
In cooperation with our long-standing partners and authorized tax and financial consultants we also offer the possibility of, aside from legal services, all tax, bookkeeping and accounting services on a monthly lump-sum payment level based on the "turnkey" and "one-stop shop" principle.
